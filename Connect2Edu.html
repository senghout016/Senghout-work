Connect2Edu is an innovative platform designed to facilitate effective and interactive learning of various skills from anywhere. At Connect2Edu, we believe that everyone should have access to high-quality education regardless of their location. Therefore, we connect the best instructors with eager students through our online education platform. Our platform provides students with access to many in-demand skills that are relevant to the Cambodian job market, ensuring that our students receive a competitive education that meets the needs of employers.

As a co-founder of this platform, I am responsible for overseeing all aspects of product design including mobile app and web app.
