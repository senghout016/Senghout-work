Local Farm KH

UX Case Study

Local Farm KH is an innovative e-commerce platform designed to support local farmers and provide fresh, high-quality produce to consumers in Cambodia. The platform aims to make it easier for consumers to access locally grown fruits and vegetables while helping farmers to reach new markets and increase their income. With the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, this platform has become even more critical, as people are seeking safe and convenient ways to purchase groceries.

I handle creating the vision, mission, and overall strategy. Managed the team to ensure the project is completed within the established timelines and budget. Also contributed to the design of the platform's user interface and user experience.

Role
Co-Founder | Project Lead | UX/UI Designer
