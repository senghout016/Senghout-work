UMpay offers a convenient and user-friendly digital asset trading platform where buying and selling digital assets is made effortless. On this platform, users have the flexibility to set their own prices, manage their transactions with ease, and access a wide network of buyers and sellers. With UMpay, users can seamlessly navigate the world of digital asset trading and take advantage of the opportunities it presents.

I use user-centered design approach to create mobile apps and landing pages. This involves understanding business requirements and utilizing design tools like user flows, wireframes, prototypes, and mockups to bring the requirements to life.
