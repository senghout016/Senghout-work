Tnaot App

Download App

TNAOT APP is an innovative platform that serves as an information aggregation and content creation platform. It is quickly becoming one of the most popular Khmer Apps, allowing users to easily access and stay up-to-date with the latest news and information. Through TNAOT, users can discover and explore a wide range of content, including news, entertainment, and lifestyle topics.

I was responsible for designing an intuitive and visually appealing interface that effectively showcases the platform's key features and content. By conducting thorough user research and creating user flows, I was able to design a user-friendly interface that makes it easy for users to navigate the app and find the content they are interested in.
